Music

I have always been fascinated with the complexity of Music. As a teenager, I would spend hours on end reading score books and analyzing the chord progressions and melodic patterns used by my favorite artists. I was curious to understand how these almost puzzle like patterns were used for creativity. This curiosity and drive to understand complexity not only extends to my interest in music but is perhaps what has also driven me to study Machine Learning as I am also passionate about technology. I am hoping to continue my Masters research in Applied Machine Learning and work on Music Generation applications and tools that can help musicians. As a guitarist or artist I feel there are still many untapped applications in which Machine Learning can be utilized to augment a players performance.



During live performances, I go by the stage name AILyra which is a combination of:

  • AI- Artificial Intelligence
  • Lyra - The star that represents the instrument of Orpheus from Greek mythology who was able to charm all living things.

    • Through the use of Neural Networks, I am in the progress of working on applications to augment my own guitar performance so please wait for more details. AI generated music is still a new concept but I am sure it will find a way to charm new audiences around the world as we continue our transformation towards the digital era. At this intersection between Technology and Music is where I have always wanted to be. Some of my sample Live performances, Photos, and Compositions can also be found here.
