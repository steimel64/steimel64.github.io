Research Interests

Applied Machine Learning

I'm interested in learning about how businesses are applying Machine Learning in our everyday lives. Many companies nowadays have achieved competitive advantage in this digital age through the use of machine learning in everyday business processes.

Machine Learning and Technology for Sustainability

As a master's student at Sophia University studying Green Science and Engineering with a dual diploma in Sustainability Science from United Nations University. I have the opportunity to take classes related to Machine Learning and Sustainability Science. I feel this cross-disciplinarity is key to solving the complex problems of the future. I always find myself thinking about how Machine Learning could make the world a better place. I am interested in understanding and applying Machine Learning to promote sustainability and smarter cities.

Deep Learning for Music

In my undergraduate days I studied music composition/classical guitar and have been a musician as long as I can remember. I have also been studying Machine Learning and recently Deep Learning has been applied to music and art generation. In the future, I hope to combine my passion for music and research Deep Learning methods applied to music generation so that I can augment my own abilities as a guitarist/composer.
  • Guitar Solo Generator
  • Neural Networks for Video Game Scoring
  • Neural Networks for backing track generation (Improvisation Tool)
